CrazyFlex X Mincraft

Facts

1. Creepers Were a Mistake

The creeper was created by accident when a pig's model was incorrectly coded!

2. Beds Explode in the Nether

Trying to sleep in the Nether or End will cause a huge explosion. It's a handy trap!

3. Enderman Speak English

Enderman sounds are distorted recordings of English phrases like “Hi” and “What’s up.”

4. Slimes Only Spawn Below Y=40

Slimes are picky. In normal worlds, they spawn below Y=40 and in slime chunks only!

5. You Can Wear Pumpkins

Wearing a carved pumpkin on your head prevents Endermen from getting angry at you.

6. Ghasts Are Voice-Acted by a Cat

The ghast sound effects were made by a sleeping cat's sounds.

7. Raining Fish

In Minecraft, you can use a bucket of tropical fish to create aquariums, or let fish 'rain' by dropping them from high.

8. Snow Golems Leave Trails

Snow golems leave a trail of snow in cold biomes.

9. Skeletons Burn in Daylight

Skeletons catch fire and burn in direct sunlight.

10. You Can Milk a Mooshroom

Mooshrooms can be milked with a bowl to get mushroom stew.

11. You Can Name a Sheep 'jeb_'

Naming a sheep 'jeb_' with a name tag will make it cycle through rainbow colors.

12. Iron Golems Offer Poppies

Iron Golems sometimes offer poppies to villagers, a reference to peaceful gestures.

13. Witches Heal Themselves

Witches can drink potions to heal themselves during fights.

14. You Can Name a Mob 'Dinnerbone'

Naming any mob 'Dinnerbone' or 'Grumm' flips them upside down.

15. Mushroom Biomes Don't Spawn Hostiles

Hostile mobs don’t naturally spawn in mushroom biomes.

16. Pandas Can Be Lazy

Pandas have different personalities, including lazy, playful, and aggressive.

17. Cows Have Been in Minecraft Forever

Cows have existed in Minecraft since version 0.0.14a.

18. Bats Serve No Purpose

Bats in Minecraft don’t drop loot or serve any real purpose—just ambiance.

19. Turtles Lay Eggs at Home

Turtles always return to the beach where they hatched to lay eggs.

20. Bees Can Pollinate Crops

Bees help speed up crop growth by pollinating them.

21. Villagers Gossip

Villagers share gossip and information, which affects iron golem spawning.

22. You Can Get Poisoned by Pufferfish

Eating a pufferfish gives you nausea, poison, and hunger.

23. Zombies Can Convert Villagers

Zombies can turn villagers into zombie villagers.

24. Zombie Pigmen Are Now Zombified Piglins

They were renamed after Nether updates.

25. Withers Drop Nether Stars

Defeating the Wither is the only way to get a Nether Star.

26. You Can Use a Compass on a Lodestone

This will make the compass point to the Lodestone.

27. Snowballs Hurt Blazes

Blazes are weak against snowballs.

28. Parrots Dance Near Jukeboxes

Parrots will dance if a jukebox is playing nearby.

29. Ravagers Are Scared of Banners

They take damage from banners during raids.

30. You Can Trade With Piglins

Throwing gold at Piglins allows you to barter.

31. You Can Walk on Powder Snow with Leather Boots

Only leather boots prevent you from falling through.

32. Fishing Has Treasure and Junk Loot Tables

Fishing can get you books, name tags, and junk.

33. You Can Cook Food on a Campfire

Campfires can cook up to 4 food items without fuel.

34. Foxes Steal Items

Foxes can spawn with or pick up items in their mouth.

35. Pillagers Can Pick Up Banners

Pillagers can carry ominous banners in raids.

36. You Can Place Water in the Nether (With Commands)

Water normally evaporates in the Nether.

37. Slime Blocks Reduce Fall Damage

Slime blocks reduce fall damage and let you bounce.

38. Honey Blocks Stick to Entities

They slow movement and can stick mobs and players.

39. You Can Use Leads on Boats

Leads can pull boats in Bedrock Edition.

40. Glow Squids Don't Glow

Glow squids emit no light—they just look like they do.

41. Axolotls Play Dead

Axolotls play dead to regenerate health.

42. Copper Blocks Oxidize Over Time

Copper changes color when exposed to air.

43. Lightning Turns Villagers Into Witches

A lightning strike can convert a villager into a witch.

44. Goats Can Knock You Off Cliffs

Goats can randomly ram players.

45. Spyglass Lets You Zoom In

You can zoom in with a spyglass to see faraway objects.

46. You Can Build a Redstone Computer

Minecraft redstone is powerful enough to simulate a computer.

47. Minecraft Was Created in 6 Days

The first version of Minecraft was coded in less than a week.

48. Steve’s Full Name Is Steve?

The default player name is just Steve—no last name confirmed.

49. Warden Is Blind

The Warden tracks players via sound and vibrations.

50. Mangrove Trees Grow in Mud

They grow in swampy areas and need mud to plant.

51. You Can Use Banners as Shields

Put banners on shields for decoration.

52. Sniffer Is an Ancient Mob

Sniffers were added from a mob vote and sniff ancient seeds.
