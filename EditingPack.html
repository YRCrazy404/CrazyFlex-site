⭐ How to Use

To get started, simply download the zip file and extract it. Import the music and effects directly into your editing app. You can also copy the fonts to your device and apply them in your projects.

  • For CapCut & VN: Tap “Import” > Select the asset
  • For KineMaster: Use “Layer” > “Media” > Add overlays or transitions
  • Fonts: Use a font installer app or manually move to your phone’s font folder
